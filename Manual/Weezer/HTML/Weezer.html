Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Weezer

On the Subject of Weezer

What's with these experts solving my mods?

This module looks like the cover to Weezer’s Blue Album. Pressing each of the members of Weezer will play a short snippet of the first verse and chorus of Buddy Holly. Identify the order that these snippets play in the song, then press the text at the top of the module to enter submission mode. To solve the module, play these clips in order. The module can’t use parts of the song that sound identical or near-identical to another part of the song.
